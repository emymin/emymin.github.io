I was interested in trying to develop a tiny game for some old console, so I ended up choosing the PS1 and made this game! I'm happy with the result, even though it doesn't have particularly amazing gameplay!


It can be played online using WASMpsx in this page, just click the "Play" button!
Alternatively, the ISO can be downloaded here to play with your own emulator (or on real hardware?)
